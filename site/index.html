Research the experts. Build the team. Ship better work.

You describe what you're building. GIGO researches the best practitioners in that field — real people with real philosophies — and builds you an expert team. Your AI plans smarter, writes better code, catches more bugs.

How It Works

Tested. Proven. Published.

We ran 9 experiments across two completely different domains — a Rails API and a children's mystery novel — to prove this actually works. It does.

  • Gets it right the first time — 97% of conventions matched on first pass, zero fixes needed
  • Senior-level output — principal engineers rated bare workers with good specs as senior to staff. Every run.
  • Better planning — expert personas caught 6 architectural gaps that bare planning missed entirely

Built on research from Gloaguen et al. and Hu et al. — then validated with our own experiments.

Why "GIGO"?

義剛 (Gigo) — "righteousness" and "strong." In computer science, GIGO means "Garbage In, Garbage Out." Both meanings apply.
